Hey there! I'm an incoming PhD student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, co-advised by Kristina Gligorić and Benjamin Van Durme. Previously, I completed my undergraduate degree at BITS Pilani, Goa Campus.
I'm interested in trustworthy AI, focusing on language models under uncertainty and multi-agent social interactions. My goal is to develop rigorous evaluations and robust methodologies that enable increasingly capable AI systems to be deployed safely and reliably in real-world settings.
I've collaborated with researchers at Stanford, Harvard, Georgia Institute of Technology, Pennsylvania State, and Emory University.
