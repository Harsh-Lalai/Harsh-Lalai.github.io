I'm an incoming PhD student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, co-advised by Kristina Gligoric and Benjamin Van Durme. Previously, I completed my undergraduate degree at BITS Pilani, Goa Campus.
My research interests span natural language processing and large language models, including work on text watermarking, geographic biases in language models, and the evaluation of vision-language models on complex reasoning tasks.
